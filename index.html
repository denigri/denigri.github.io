Rationale Coffee Roasters

Rationale Coffee Roasters is an Italian micro roastery and a retail. Specialty coffee market is growing significantly in Italy. And even though Italy invented the word barista ☕️, the quality of roasting and the ways how coffee is served and prepared are too low. The request was to codify and visualize the idea of the perfect coffee, the right preparation and the required knowledge to enjoy it.
